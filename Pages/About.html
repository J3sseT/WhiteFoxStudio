About Me

Hey there! I’m Jo– a photographer & graphic designer based in a small town near Berlin, Germany – and I am so excited for you to be here!

I have enjoyed and experimented with Camera all my life, but it wasn’t until I went to the US as an EduCare that I realized my passion for

creative work runs more profound than for it to be just a hobby.


While being an EduCare, I visited a local College and fell in love with the photography classes.

Since then, I have been photographing different events and portraits and working with various clients on studio projects.


I like to think I take a different approach when shooting content. Good photographers come into a project knowing the type of shots they want.

While this is important, I’ve learned to take what we’ve discussed and creatively develop a story to harmonize with your unique vision.

The goal is to capture everything in its purest form for that I catch the essence of the moment forever.


I tackle each project with impeccable care and a meticulous eye for detail to ensure that each dimension of your vision,

dream, and memorable day is brought to life… I’m lucky to have something that brings so much happiness into my life,

and this is my small way of sharing that happiness back with the world.
